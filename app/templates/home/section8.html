Tips & Troubleshooting

Common questions and helpful tips to get the most out of our document splitting service.

For Best Results

  • Use proper heading styles in your document (Heading 1, Heading 2, etc.)
  • Include a table of contents for more accurate splitting
  • Use consistent formatting throughout your document
  • Ensure your document is not password-protected

Troubleshooting

  • If splitting fails, check if your document is corrupted
  • Try saving your document as a newer DOCX version
